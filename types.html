Types of Massage

I will generally use a combination of all these techniques.

  • Neuro-Muscular Therapy: Specific work on a particular muscle, usually deep.
  • Deep Tissue: Going beyond the first layers of skin into the muscle, usually long strokes.
  • Swedish: Long, gentle stokes along the body with in the first few layers of skin, used for relaxation.
  • Myo-fascial Release: Separating the top layer of skin from the underlying tissue.
  • Reflexology: Working with the body’s systems on the foot itself.
  • Stretching & Exercises: Learn techniques for stretching problem “strong” muscles; along with strengthening problem “weak” muscles. To be done daily.
