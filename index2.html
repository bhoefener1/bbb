Therapeutic Massage:

By stretching and loosening muscles and connective tissues in your body, massage therapy improves the circulatory and immune systems, plus delivers many other benefits:

  • Reduces Stress and can lower Blood Pressure
  • Relieves Headaches
  • Increases General Health
  • Increases your feeling of well-being and prevents injuries
  • Relieve back and shoulder/neck pain and reduce feelings of fibromyalgia
Other Important Elements:

During a session I explain to my clients the importance of stretching/ strengthening, posture, and water.

Stretching/ Strengthening

keeps your body going by using the majority of the muscles that are not usually touched by a person's average routine, this way they do not decrease into atrophy. It also lengthens the muscles that are consistently shortened throughout the day. Example: Your hamstrings- if you sit at all- you are shortening the distance from your knee to your pelvis. If your body does not lengthen this muscle it will continuously shorten, this is one of a few elements involved with low back pain. Go to the stretch and strengthen section of my newsletters page. Strengthening is also just as important because as there are tight strong muscles in your body there are tight weak muscles there as well. You need to have a balance or your body will be compensate, which will lead to your pain. A good example of this is the upper leg with knee problems. Most people have strong quadriceps and outer thigh, with weak hamstrings and inner thigh. This makes it so every step taken tracks the knee to the outer side of the leg, making your knee hurt after some walking/ running, it may even develop slight inflammation.
