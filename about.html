In addition to computer technical skills, I believe I've learned how to be an efficient time manager, how to work effectively with others, and how to "get the job done" in a timely and professional manner.

Why should I hire you?

Often when businesses need last-minute help with an unexpected or unplanned project, they may find themselves without the resources on hand to complete it immediately. There may also be some businesses that are not yet prepared to hire a full time employee, yet could benefit greatly from outsourcing certain tasks on an "as-needed" or on a regular basis.

Prices for services are reasonable and you are assured of quality when your request for services is rendered and responded to. You will never be quoted on a job that cannot be fulfilled to your expectations. You will never have to pay for 8 hours of an employee sitting idle or unable to complete the task. You will receive a fair quote and estimate beforehand.
